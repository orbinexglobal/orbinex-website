My Process

A clear, step-by-step workflow to ensure a smooth and successful project.

01 Discovery

We start with a detailed discussion to understand your goals, needs, and project requirements.

* Clear communication and understanding is key.

02 Proposal & Agreement

I will provide a clear proposal outlining the scope of work, timeline, and cost. Once agreed, we will formalize it with a straightforward agreement.

* Transparent pricing and project scope. 30% deposit required to begin.

03 Design Approval

I will provide initial designs/mockups for your review and feedback.

* Revision Limits: Two rounds of revisions included to refine the design.

04 Development

Upon approval, I will begin delivering your project, keeping you updated throughout the process.

05 Delivery

Once everything is finalized and you are happy with the result, I will launch your website and provide all the necessary files.

06 Maintenance (Optional)

If you opt for ongoing maintenance, I will provide continuous support to keep your site updated and performing well.
